Bolognese

Description

A warm embrace from Italy, just like momma used to make.

A savory spaghetti dish, topped with ground beef and tomato sauce and garnished with basil.

It's certain to make you want to visit the Italian countryside.

Ingredients:

  • ground beef
  • olive oil
  • diced onion and garlic
  • spaghetti
  • a can of diced tomatoes
  • salt and pepper
  • sugar
  • spices to your liking
  • fresh basil leaf
  1. Pour the olive oil, onion and garlic on a frying pan
  2. After they cook for a bit, add the beef
  3. When the beef is mostly cooked, add the can of tomatoes and the spices
  4. Add a bit of water and let it simmer on low heat
  5. In another pan, add water and salt and bring it to a boil
  6. Whenever it's boiling, add the pasta and let it cook until al dente
  7. Serve your delicious bolognese pasta and garnish with basil or grated cheese
